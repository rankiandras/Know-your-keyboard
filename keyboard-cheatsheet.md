Brackets

()  shift+9     round brackets aka parenthesis
[]  ő           square brackets aka box brackets
{}  shift+ő     curly brackets aka curly braces
<>  í           angle brackets aka chevrons

Quotation marks

'   á           single quote
"   shift+á     double quote
`   0           backtick

Slashes and bar

/   -           slash
\   ű           backslash
|   shift+ű     bar

#   shift+3     hash

%   shift+5     percent
&   shift+7     ampersand
*   shift+8     asterisk
-   ü           dash
